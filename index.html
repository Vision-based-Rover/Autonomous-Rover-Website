What is AWR?

Physical Robot

A differential-drive ground vehicle assembled from off-the-shelf components — Raspberry Pi 4, L298N motor driver, VL53L0X ToF sensors, and MPU-6050 IMU. Total BOM cost is a fraction of commercial AGV systems.

Raspberry Pi 4 Differential Drive

Onboard RL Policy

A PPO policy trained via curriculum learning runs directly on the Raspberry Pi at 15 Hz. No cloud dependency — the robot reasons locally from sensor data and acts in real time.

PPO v6.2 15 Hz Local Inference

Safety-First Design

A deterministic Safety Guard Node overrides RL commands whenever braking distance constraints are violated — guaranteeing zero collisions regardless of policy edge cases.

Zero Collisions Hard Override
