// INDUSTRIAL_RELEVANCE

Cost Reduction

Modern warehouses rely on manual labor or multi-million dollar AGV systems. This RL-based rover uses low-cost ToF and IR sensors to achieve 92% navigation accuracy.

Safety-First AI

By implementing a Safety Guard Node, the system clips unsafe RL commands based on real-time braking distance, ensuring zero-collision operation in human-centric aisles.
